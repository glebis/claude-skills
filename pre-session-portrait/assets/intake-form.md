# Pre-session portrait — live intake form

*For interviewing a client live (or by voice notes). One open question per lens; jot the compressed `capture:` line as you go.*

### 0 · ANCHOR — what did you book this call to work on?
> One sentence. Everything below hangs off this.

`capture:` *the topic*

### 1 · WHERE — where are you with that right now?
> What have you already tried, and where does it stall?

`capture:` *what's been tried + where it stalls*

### 2 · HOW — how do you think?
> When you work a hard problem like this, how does your mind move? Fast/slow, visual/verbal, systems/stories, top-down/bottom-up?

`capture:` *cognitive style*

### 3 · WHAT — what's on your mind?
> What are you preoccupied with lately — the open loops, the recurring thought?

`capture:` *live preoccupation*

### 4 · PROBLEM — what's the real problem?
> The problem under the problem. Fix one thing — what ache stops?

`capture:` *the core job*

### 5 · IDEAL — what does "solved" look like?
> The better version, concretely. Not the tool — the day, the feeling.

`capture:` *desired outcome*

### 6 · TENSION — what's the pull the other way?
> What holds you back, worries you, makes you hesitate?

`capture:` *dominant anxiety/tension*

### 7 · JTBD — the forces
> - **Push** (frustrating now): ______
> - **Pull** (attracts to the new): ______
> - **Habit** (comfortable about staying): ______
> - **Anxiety** (makes change risky): ______
> - **Trigger** (why now): ______

`capture:` *4 force-words + trigger → Push/Pull vs Habit/Anxiety grid*
